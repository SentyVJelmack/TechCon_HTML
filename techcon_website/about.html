History of TechCon

TechCon started in 2010 as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts and has grown into one of the leading tech conferences worldwide.

Our Mission

TechCon aims to foster innovation, learning, and networking opportunities for tech professionals and enthusiasts alike.

Past Notable Speakers

Ada Lovelace

A pioneering mathematician and writer, known as one of the first computer programmers.

Alan Turing

Computer scientist and cryptanalyst, widely regarded as the father of theoretical computer science and artificial intelligence.

Grace Hopper

Computer scientist and US Navy rear admiral, known for her work on programming languages and software development.
